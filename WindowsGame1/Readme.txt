Instructions: Left and Right arrow keys to move; or Joystick on Xbox. Use the tild key (`) on the keyboard to bring up the console, use Esc to leave.
              Space or Xbox "A" to start/resume the game and P or Xbox "Start" to pause.

Console commands: BACKGROUND RED/GREEN/BLUE will change the background respectively
		  BRICK ADD/MINUS will add/subtract a row of bricks
